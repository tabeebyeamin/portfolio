About Me

Who am I?

BIO

I am a Bengali-Canadian dude who likes to do computer stuff. As you may have read from my headline, I like programming, making music and playing video games. I am a second year Computer Science Student at the University of Toronto. I also love to teach people about the things I learn. I teach students about Computer Science concepts as a Computer Science Representative in AMACSS at my University. I taught students Calculus II on my TYEducational YouTube. They say the best way to master material is to teach it to be able to teach it to someone else, and I firmly believe that!
